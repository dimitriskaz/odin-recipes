Greek Salad Recipe

Description

A traditional Greek salad, known as Horiatiki is a simple yet flavorful dish that showcases the f reshness of Mediterranean ingredients. To prepare this classic salad, start by cutting ripe tomatoes into wedges and slicing cucumbers into half-moons. Add thin slices of red onion and crisp green bell peppers for extra crunch. Then, include a handful of Kalamata olives, which bring a rich, briny flavor. The salad is typically topped with a generous block of feta cheese, rather than crumbled, giving it a signature Greek touch.

Season the salad with dried oregano, sea salt, and a drizzle of high-quality extra virgin olive oil. For a bit of tang, add a splash of red wine vinegar. The salad is traditionally served without lettuce, allowing the bold flavors of the vegetables and cheese to shine. It's best enjoyed fresh, either on its own or as a side dish, alongside crusty bread to soak up the delicious juices.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • Handful of Kalamata olives
  • Block of feta cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Sea salt to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar

Steps

  1. Cut the tomatoes into wedges and slice the cucumber into half-moons.
  2. Thinly slice the red onion and green bell pepper.
  3. Add the tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and bell pepper to a large salad bowl.
  4. Toss in the Kalamata olives.
  5. Place a block of feta cheese on top of the salad.
  6. Season with dried oregano and sea salt.
  7. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and a splash of red wine vinegar.
  8. Serve immediately with crusty bread on the side.
